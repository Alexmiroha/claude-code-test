# Buy now or wait?

This is one of the most common questions in online shopping. The price looks good, the promotion ends today, and the store shows a message like **“last units available”**. But is it really worth buying right now?

## When it makes sense to buy now

Buying now makes sense if at least two or three of these conditions are true:

- the price is the lowest it has been in a long time,
- the product has limited availability,
- you need it now,
- the product rarely goes on sale,
- the difference versus the usual price is really meaningful,
- the seller is reliable,
- returns and warranty are straightforward.

If the price is good, the product is needed and the seller is trustworthy, buying now can be a reasonable decision.

## When it is better to wait

It is better to wait if:

- the product is not urgent,
- the promotion looks average,
- the price often drops,
- Black Friday, Cyber Monday or a seasonal sale is coming soon,
- a new model is expected soon,
- the product has mixed reviews,
- you are not sure whether you really need it.

The worst purchases are the ones we make only because a promotion is “ending soon”.

## Products that often get cheaper

Some categories regularly drop in price.

This often applies to:

- electronics,
- smartphones,
- laptops,
- TVs,
- headphones,
- computer accessories,
- small home appliances,
- seasonal clothing,
- gaming products.

With these products, patience often pays off.

## Products worth buying when the price is good

Some products do not always get large discounts. If you find a genuinely good price, it can make sense to buy faster.

Examples:

- popular models with strong reviews,
- products with limited availability,
- equipment that rarely gets discounted,
- everyday essentials,
- products you already planned to buy.

In these cases, waiting for the perfect deal may not make sense.

## The simple 24-hour rule

If you are not sure, use the 24-hour rule.

Do not buy immediately. Add the product to your cart or wishlist and come back the next day.

After 24 hours, ask yourself:

- Do I still want this product?
- Do I really need it?
- Does the price still look good?
- Have I found a better offer?
- Am I buying the product, or just the emotion of the promotion?

This is a simple way to avoid impulse purchases.

## Quick checklist

Buy now if:

- the price is genuinely good,
- you need the product,
- the seller is reliable,
- return terms are good,
- you checked other stores.

Wait if:

- the product is not urgent,
- the price is not exceptional,
- the promotion looks artificial,
- bigger sales may be coming soon,
- you are not sure about the purchase.

## Summary

The best shopping decision is not always “buy now”. Sometimes the best deal is **not buying anything under pressure**.
